TREATMENT:

How We Classify Risk

Your heart risk isn’t “high or low.” It’s a spectrum.
We classify every member into one of four categories:

• Low Risk — maintain and optimize
• Moderate Risk — targeted interventions
• High Risk — aggressive medical therapy

This allows us to deliver the right plan, not a generic one.



Your Personalized Treatment Recommendation

Each Bypass plan is built by cardiologists and driven by data — not templates.

1. Foundation Plan
For low–moderate risk individuals
• Lifestyle optimization
• Medication if indicated
• Monthly follow-ups
• Wearable + BP monitoring
• Nutrition / fitness program

2. Intensive Medical Plan
For moderate–high risk individuals
• Targeted medical therapy (lipids, BP, glucose)
• Imaging-based decision-making
• VO₂ max improvement program
• Medication titration
• Frequent monitoring and adjustments

3. High-Risk Intervention Plan
For high–very high risk
• Aggressive lipid + BP control
• CTA/CAC-guided therapy
• Close cardiology supervision
• Rapid follow-up cycles
• Advanced risk reversal strategies



Why This Plan Works

Every recommendation is derived from global cardiovascular outcome studies — and validated in Indian populations.

Your plan is designed to:

• Lower ApoB by 20–60%
• Reduce blood pressure by 5–15 mmHg
• Improve VO₂ max by 10–25%
• Reduce inflammation
• Slow or reverse plaque progression
• Cut long-term risk by up to 40%
